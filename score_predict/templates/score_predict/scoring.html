Scoring Rules

Welcome to the football prediction game! Points are awarded for both the accuracy of your scoreline predictions and your ability to judge the overall match result. Read below to see how you can rack up points and edge out the competition!

Main Scoring System

  • 10 points – Predict the exact score (e.g., 2–1 when the final score is 2–1).
  • 5 points – Predict the correct result only (home win, draw, or away win), but not the exact score.

Tie-Breaker: Alternate Scoring System

If two or more players finish with the same score, we use the alternate scoring system to decide the winner. This system rewards accuracy in smaller details of your prediction.

1. Result Prediction (3 points)

Earn 3 points if you correctly predict whether the game ends in a home win, draw, or away win.
Predict incorrectly? You score 0.

2. Home Team Goals (up to 3 points)

Predict the number of goals scored by the home team. You start with 3 points for a perfect prediction, but lose 1 point for every goal you’re off.

Examples:
Predict 3 goals and they score 3 → 3 points
Predict 2 goals and they score 1 → 2 points
Predict 4 goals and they score 0 → 0 points

3. Away Team Goals (up to 3 points)

Exactly the same system applies to the away team’s goals.

Maximum Points Available:
3 (Result) + 3 (Home Goals) + 3 (Away Goals) = 9 points.
But… if you get the exact correct scoreline, you earn a bonus point, making it a total of 10 points!

Final Word

This system rewards both bold and precise predictions. Go for the exact score to hit the jackpot, but remember—even being close counts!
